2025 Festival Highlights

Join us for our annual celebration of mathematics, technology, and community. This year's festival features exciting competitions, exhibitions, and games for all students.

Male students compete in a timed cooking challenge using everyday ingredients!

Ladies' Cook-Off

Female students showcase culinary creativity in a friendly battle.

ICT Talent Exhibition

Display your animations, apps, or tech demos. Prizes for innovation!

Games & Sports Carnival

Indoor (chess, ludu) and outdoor (football, sack races) games for all.
